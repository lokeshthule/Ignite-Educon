About Ignite Educon

Ignite Educon has a rich legacy of transforming education through innovation, collaboration, and mentorship. From our early beginnings as IIT Pratishthan to becoming a dedicated center of excellence, we continue to inspire and guide students to achieve their dreams.

2007 – The Beginning

18 coaching units from various locations of Maharashtra joined hands to bring a standardized, better coaching system under a single unified brand — IIT Pratishthan.

Trusted by Maharashtra’s Leading Colleges

  • Pune Region: Fergusson College, MES Abasaheb Garware College
  • Mumbai & Thane Region: Vivekanand College, Kirti College, Dnyansadhana (Thane), Pendharkar College (Dombiwali), Thakur College (Virar)
  • Marathwada & Vidarbha: Deogiri College (Sambhaji Nagar), Saraswati College (Sambhaji Nagar), Dayanand (Latur), KTHM College (Nasik)
  • Western & Southern Maharashtra: Wellington College (Sangli), Samgmeshwar College (Solapur)

2012 – Expansion

Opened international venues through tie-ups in Muscat, Sultanate of Oman. In the Gulf region, we primarily focused on SAT Subjective Coaching.

2014–15 – New Era

In 2014, a new management team emerged with a core approach of working with talent and not masses. Soon, the IIT Pratishthan brand was handed over to Ignite Educon.

Since 2015, we have been operating from a dedicated location for higher learning of JEE Advanced, both in regular and integrated formats, at MTES Jr. College, off Karve Road. This morning batch model provides students with more time for self-study and self-development.

2021 – YTS Foundation

The Yeshwant Talent Search (YTS) was inspired by Late Dr. Yeshwant R. Waghmare, a distinguished physicist and former Dean of IIT Kanpur. After returning from MIT, USA, he dedicated himself to guiding and uplifting young talent in India. His mentorship enabled 16 out of 19 students to secure admission to IITs, proving his unmatched clarity and vision.

One of his mentees, Dr. Shantanu Deshpande—an academician, career counsellor, and coaching expert—joined him in 2014, and together they began an innovative journey that continues today under Ignite Educon.
